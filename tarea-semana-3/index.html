Omelette Recipe
Classic Omelette
Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

4 large eggs

room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

2 tablespoon unsalted butter

Instructions

Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.

Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.

Pur in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.

Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs fron the edge of the skillet toward...

When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half.

Slide the omelette onto a plate.
